Start a Science Hack Day PDX Team!

Anyone can propose an idea for a team. Your team doesn't even have to exist when the event starts, or even have a project concept yet. You can figure out everything at the beginning of the event.

If you want to start finding or organizing a team effort now, you can follow these two steps:

  • Recruit some friends and register tickets
  • Join our Slack and introduce yourself and/or your project or team
